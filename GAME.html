If the 3D game are slow , so to optimize the 3D performance:
* resize the window game from full screen to small screen.
* Go to :Control Panel\Appearance and Personalization\Personalization & select theme from:
Basic and High Contrast Themes; so change desktop theme to other with low quality.
* Use a PC with high performance (procesor & RAM) OR add graphic card to your PC.
